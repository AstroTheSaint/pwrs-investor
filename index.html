The Opportunity

Market Size

The global live music industry is projected to reach $31.5 billion by 2026, with a CAGR of 3.5% from 2021 to 2026.

Growth Potential

Post-pandemic surge in live events, combined with increasing demand for data-driven tour management solutions.

Target Market

Focusing on mid-tier artists ($50k-$500k per show) who lack sophisticated tour management tools.

Platform Overview

For Artists

  • Track real-time revenue and expenses
  • Manage tour schedules and logistics
  • Receive AI-powered venue recommendations
  • Access detailed analytics and insights

For Agents

  • Manage multiple artists efficiently
  • Track negotiations and offers
  • Access venue compatibility matching
  • Monitor payment processing and revenue
